# Bridge-to-Perfect Gap Matrix (6 Personas)

Source baseline: `docs/real-life-6-persona-evaluation-2026-03-27.md`

## Equal-Weight Acceptance Contract

- Every section is equally critical.
- No section is accepted unless all 6 personas rate it `>= 4.5/5`.
- Final loop acceptance requires all 6 personas rate every section `>= 4.9/5`.
- Any miss reopens the section for redesign plus full re-test.

## Evidence Required per Iteration

- Score table for all 6 personas and all sections.
- Behavior-change notes per section (what is visibly improved in product behavior).
- Objective metrics deltas (onboarding timing, completion, mentor impact, evaluation stability).
- Decision memo: continue / re-open / block.

## Stop Rule

Do not mark any element as acceptable until all six personas score that element at or above the active gate for that cycle.
